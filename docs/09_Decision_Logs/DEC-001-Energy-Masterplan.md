# DEC-001: Energy Masterplan Initial Design Basis

## Status

Accepted

## Date

2026-07-04

## Decision

Design infrastructure for 100 kW solar expansion but initially model 50 kW solar + 100 kWh battery.

## Context

The site has a known average power bill of approximately $1,200 per month and known consumption of approximately 93 kWh/day average. Workday loads are likely higher than the average due to workshop activity.

Designing only for the first installation may create avoidable rework later. However, immediately installing the full future system may not be justified without interval data, supplier quotes, finance options, and a more complete load profile.

## Rationale

- Stage 1 can reduce bills while limiting upfront commitment.
- The design should avoid blocking later solar expansion.
- Battery sizing should be tested against actual interval data.
- Future loads may include dam pumping, EV charging, and additional workshop capacity.

## Consequences

- Quotes should request a Stage 1 price and future expansion pathway.
- Switchboard, inverter, battery, and cabling decisions should consider the future 100 kW target.
- Financial modelling should initially focus on 50 kW solar + 100 kWh battery.
