# Finance Options Register

## Purpose

Track possible finance pathways for the Stage 1 solar and battery project.

This register is a planning document. Each option must be confirmed before it is used in the business case.

| Option | Status | Potential use | Key questions | Next step |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Government interest-free loans | Requires investigation | Could make finance clearly better than using offset cash | Does a current commercial solar/battery loan exist? Is the property or business eligible? | Verify current federal, Queensland, council, and program-specific options |
| CEFC-backed green finance | Requires investigation | May support lower-rate clean energy finance through participating lenders | Which lenders offer it? Does it cover batteries and commercial sites? | Ask banks and brokers for CEFC-backed options |
| Bank green business loans | Requires investigation | Could fund solar, battery, and related equipment | Rate, term, fees, security, early repayment, eligible equipment | Ask existing bank and at least two alternatives |
| Solar retailer finance | Unconfirmed | Convenient quote-linked finance | Is the headline rate hiding fees or inflated system price? | Ask each solar quote provider for cash price and financed price |
| Equipment finance | Requires investigation | May suit business-owned energy assets | Is solar/battery eligible? What is the effective rate and tax treatment? | Ask accountant and finance broker |
| Chattel mortgage | Requires investigation | May suit business asset purchase if ownership and tax treatment align | Is the asset structure appropriate? How are GST and depreciation handled? | Ask accountant before relying on this |
| Business loan | Unconfirmed | General funding pathway if green products are unsuitable | Does the rate beat the offset-adjusted case? What security is required? | Compare against green finance and cash purchase |

## Notes For Comparison

- Always compare finance against the home loan offset benchmark.
- Ask for the effective annual rate, not only the advertised rate.
- Include establishment fees, account fees, early repayment fees, and security costs.
- Confirm whether interest is deductible.
- Confirm who owns the asset during the finance term.
- Separate solar STCs, battery incentives, and finance discounts in every quote.
