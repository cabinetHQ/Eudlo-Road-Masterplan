## Federal STCs For Solar

Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs) may reduce the upfront installed cost of eligible solar systems.

Actions:

- Confirm eligibility for the proposed system size and site type.
- Ask each solar supplier to show STC treatment clearly in the quote.
- Compare gross cost and net cost after STCs.

## Federal Battery Incentive

Federal battery incentives should be investigated and tracked as part of the battery investment case.

Actions:

- Confirm eligibility rules.
- Confirm approved battery and inverter requirements.
- Check whether the incentive applies to commercial, residential, or mixed-use properties.
- Ask suppliers to separate battery incentive assumptions from solar STCs.

## Queensland MEEG Grant

The Queensland Manufacturing Energy Efficiency Grant (MEEG) should be considered for workshop and manufacturing efficiency upgrades.

Important exclusions:

- Solar panels are excluded.
- Batteries are excluded.
- Second-hand equipment is excluded.
- Switchboard upgrades are excluded.

Potentially useful MEEG-aligned items:

- Compressor upgrades
- Variable speed drives (VSDs)
- Efficient motors
- LED lighting upgrades
- Energy monitoring
- Automatic shut-off controls

## ARENA Advancing Renewables

ARENA Advancing Renewables is likely unsuitable for an ordinary solar installation.

It may only be relevant if the project is framed as a genuine renewable energy demonstration project with broader innovation, replication, or industry learning value.

## R&D Tax Incentive

The R&D Tax Incentive should be investigated for BoundHQ and related development work.

Potential areas to assess:

- Manufacturing automation
- AI workflow improvements
- Process optimisation
- Novel production methods
- Internal software or operational systems with genuine experimental uncertainty

Next step: speak to accountant or R&D adviser before relying on this in the investment case.
